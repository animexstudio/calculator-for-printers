This illustration shows approximate sizes relative to a standard 90-inch sofa:

Tips for choosing the right print size:

  • Match your image's aspect ratio to the closest standard ratio for best results
  • For wall art, larger prints (16+ inches) typically work best when viewed from 5+ feet away
  • Consider the viewing distance - higher DPI is needed for closer viewing
  • For framing, remember to account for mat and frame dimensions beyond the print size
